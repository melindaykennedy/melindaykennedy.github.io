Yummy Pork Chops

Total Time 30 min. Makes 1 doz. Author SexySerina

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups italian-style salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 boneless pork chops

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a bowl, mix the italian-style salad dressing, soy sauce, and pepper.
  2. Place the pork chops in a skillet over meadium heat, and cover with the dressing mixture.
  3. Cover skillet, and coook pork chops 25 minutes, turning occasionally.
  4. Remove cover, reduce heat to low, and continue cooking to desired doneness.

Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp

Total Time 30 min. Makes 8 servings Author KimmyK

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 tablespoons cajun seasoning (your choice)
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined medium shrimp

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix together cajun seasoning, lime juice, and oil in a reuseable plastic bag.
  2. Add shrimp, coat with marinade, and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Cook shrimp on grill until bright pink on the outside and it is no longer transparent in the center, aboutr 2 minutes on each side.

Sweet and Spicy Gochujang Chicken

Total Time 30 min. Makes 6 servings Author Sarah Brekke

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/3 cup gochujang (korean hot pepper paste)
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sweet rice wine (mirin)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 teaspoon seasame oil, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 3 tablespoons thinly bias-sliced
  • 6 cups cooked rice
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneles chicken breats, cut into pices

DIRECTIONS

  1. Stir together gochujang, soy sauce, sweet rice wine, honey, garlic, sesame oil, and ginger in a small bowl.
  2. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a wok (or large skillet), add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Remove all chicken from wok. Stir sauce well and add to the wok. Cook until thick and bubbly.
  4. Once sauce is thick and bubbly, add chicken and cook until hot.
  5. Top with sesame seeds and green onions. Serve immediately with cooked rice.
