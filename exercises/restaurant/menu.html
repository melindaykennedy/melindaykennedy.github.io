Cap'n Crunch Berry Pancakes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups pancake mix
  • 1-1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup Cap'n Crunch Crunch Berries, crushed
  • 1/8 cup Cap'n Crunch Crunch Berries, crushed (red berries only)
  • 1/8 cup Cap'n Crunch Crunch Berries, crushed (green berries only)
  • 1/8 cup Cap'n Crunch Crunch Berries, crushed (blue berries only)
  • 1/8 cup Cap'n Crunch Crunch Berries, crushed (purple berries only)
  • 6 drops red food coloring
  • 6 drops blue food coloring
  • 3/4 cup white chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoon heavy whipping cream

DIRECTIONS

  1. Combine pancake mix with water.
  2. Separate pancake mix evenly into four cups.
  3. Add a different food coloring into each cup and mix until fully combined. (use red and blue food coloring to make a purple color.)
  4. Mix the crushed up berries into their respective colored pancake mix.
  5. Melt the white chocolate chips in the microwave. Mix in powdered sugar and heaving whipping cream. Set aside.
  6. Make each colored pancake and spread the white chocolate glaze in between each layer, adding the original Cap'n flavor as you stack pancakes.
  7. Heavily spread the white chocolate glaze on the top pancake and sprinkle with the rest of the crushed Cap'n Crunch cereal.
  8. NOW YOU CAN DIG IN!!!!!
